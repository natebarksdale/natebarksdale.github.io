When declining to contribute to New York's Museum of Modern Art, Gertrude Stein [commented](https://www.google.com/search?q=%22commented%22%20thebrowser.com) "You can be a museum, or you can be modern, but you can't be both." Who knows if that's universally true, but this video (really a series of stills) for me triggers not the bracing feelings of novel modernity but rather a pleasant nostalgia. I've never been inside MoMA, but seeing so many famously familiar works of art makes it feel like coming home.
