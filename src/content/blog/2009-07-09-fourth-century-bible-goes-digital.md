> ![image](http://culture-making.com/media/codex_210.jpg)The Codex Sinaiticus, the oldest extant copy of the Bible, has been digitized by the Codex Sinaiticus Project, and can now be viewed online [here](http://www.codexsinaiticus.org/en/project/). The manuscript contains the entire New Testament, and most of the Old Testament, all in Greek (the original language of the New Testament).
