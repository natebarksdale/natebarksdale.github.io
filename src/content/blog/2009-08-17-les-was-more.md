Last week guitar and recording pioneer Les Paul passed away at age 94. While I knew he'd had something to do (in parallel with Leo Fender and Adolph Rickenbacker) with the development of the solid-body electric guitar, I didn't realize that he had a still-more-solid claim as the father of multitrack recording.
