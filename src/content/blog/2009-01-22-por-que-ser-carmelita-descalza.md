This is a promotional video from a 400-year-old convent of the "Barefoot Carmelite" order in Ecija, Spain, near Seville. They were down to 11 nuns and had had no new novices join for three years, so they decided to try something drastic: YouTube. The video has generated [lots of interest](http://www.guardian.co.uk/world/2009/jan/16/nuns-turn-to-youtube-for-recruits), and their first YouTube-inspired novice has just joined the order.
